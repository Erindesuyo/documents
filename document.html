1. The state or quality of being infinite.

2. 무한: 수, 양, 공간, 시간 따위에 제한이나 끝이 없음.

Library Of Babel
The Library of Babel symbolizes infinite truths that we cannot attain. Although humans can never reach the end, they do not give up and strive to find meaning within it. Ultimately, this space serves as a stage that visually demonstrates 'the fate of humans living today yearning for the impossible.'
